Assesment Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully

  • Do not reload the page during the assessment. Reloading the page at any point will automatically reset your score to 0%, and the system will redirect you, resulting in loss of progress.

  • Ensure all questions are answered within the allotted time. Failure to complete all questions within the specified time frame may affect your final score.

Note: Scores below the required threshold of 70% will not be considered satisfactory for evaluation as a tutor.
