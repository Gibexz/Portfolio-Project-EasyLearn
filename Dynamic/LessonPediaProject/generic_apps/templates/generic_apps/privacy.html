LessonPedia Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to LessonPedia, your trusted online learning platform that connects learners and tutors. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled safely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, disclosure, and protection of your information.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Personal Information

When you register on our platform, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, and contact number. If you are a tutor, we may also collect information about your qualifications, experience, and areas of expertise.

2.2 Payment Information

We collect transactional information and payment details when you make a payment on LessonPedia. We use secure payment gateways for transactions, and we do not store your credit card information.

2.3 Usage Information

We collect information about your usage of our platform, including the courses you view or enroll in, the lessons you complete, your interactions with tutors and other learners, and your feedback and ratings.

2.4 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information, including IP addresses, device information, browser type, and usage patterns, to improve user experience, provide support, and analyze platform usage.

2.5 Communications

We may collect information from communications between users and tutors on our platform, including messages, feedback, and session records.

3. Use of Your Information

3.1 Providing Our Services

We use your personal information to provide our services, process your transactions, and communicate with you about your account, course enrollments, and changes to our policies.

3.2 Improving Our Platform

We use the usage information we collect to understand how our platform is used, to improve our services, and to develop new features and functionalities.

3.3 Marketing and Promotions

With your consent, we may use your personal information to send you newsletters, promotional materials, and other information that may be of interest to you. You can opt out of receiving these communications at any time.

4. Information Sharing

4.1 Service Providers

We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, email delivery, and customer service.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law, in response to a court order, legal process, or government request, or to enforce our policies or protect the rights, property, or safety of LessonPedia, its users, or others.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures, including data encryption, secure servers, and firewalls, to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can also opt out of receiving marketing communications from us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at developers.lessonpedia@gmail.com.

Consent
By using LessonPedia, users agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
